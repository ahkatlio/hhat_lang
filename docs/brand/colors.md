## Accessibility

All color combinations meet **WCAG 2.1 Level AA** standards for contrast:

**Light Theme**:
- Text on background: 7.8:1 ratio (AAA ✓✓✓)
- Primary on background: 5.2:1 ratio (AA ✓✓)

**Dark Theme**:
- Text on background: 10.1:1 ratio (AAA ✓✓✓)
- Primary on background: 7.9:1 ratio (AAA ✓✓✓)

## Design Principles

1. **No pure black or white**: Reduces eye strain and looks more sophisticated
2. **Warm tones**: Cream background in light mode feels welcoming
3. **Cool tones**: Deep blue in dark mode is calming
4. **Quantum distinction**: Teal colors consistently represent quantum elements
5. **High contrast**: All text is easily readable
6. **Color blind friendly**: Palette works for common color vision deficiencies
